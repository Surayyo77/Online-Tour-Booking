ABOUT THE TOUR

ㅤㅤㅤ

Germany reveals itself in layers — from the electric pulse of Berlin's Mitte to the velvet green silence of the Black Forest.

ㅤㅤㅤ

Our 10-day luxury tour is crafted for travelers who seek meaning beyond the itinerary: conversations with local artisans, meals that carry centuries of tradition, and views so breathtaking they render guidebooks unnecessary.

ㅤㅤㅤ

Small groups. Private guides. Unmeasurable memories. Every hotel is hand-selected, every restaurant vetted, every moment designed to feel serendipitous.

DAYS 1-2

Berlin

Brandenburg Gate, Museum Island, Checkpoint Charlie, and the vibrant Kreuzberg art scene.
